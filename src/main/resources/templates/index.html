What Our Cutomers Says

Jens Madsen

"Best way of reducing food waste"
I was passing through and saw the sign from the road and I could not believe my eyes. This place is tucked into one of those little malls. GoGreen is one of my favorite things in the world so a quick turn of the steering wheel and in I went. I was not disappointed. There were so many kinds, even more than my grandma made so I order the GoGreen Mealplan 1 that had one of everything including side salads of tabouli and hummus. That was to go. For eating right there I had the falafel salad which was so tasty and fresh and used ingredients of very high quality. I wish this place was closer to home. I would be a regular.
